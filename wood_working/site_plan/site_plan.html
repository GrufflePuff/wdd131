Overview

Purpose

To provide detailed information on different types of wood allowing a person to understand their characteristics and unique traits to help them select the appropriate wood for each project.

Audience

Professional and hobbiest woodworkers.

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements will be blocks that dynamically display the different types of woods and their characteristics. Each different type of wood will have its own info block.
