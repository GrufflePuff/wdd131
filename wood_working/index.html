Home Page

The purpose of this website is to provide detailed explanations on the properties of various woods species. It is useful to have an understanding of the material you use to craft with, our goal is to provide information for woodworkers working with unfamiliar materials. By comparing the various properties of materials it can help decide on what material you'd use for a project and help you understand how a material might act.
